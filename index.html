Motivational Tips

pomofocus

Set a timer for 25 minutes of focused study followed by a 5-minute break.

Do Not Disturb (iOS)

Turn off notifications while studying to avoid distractions.

Do Not Disturb (Android)

Use a phone’s "Do Not Disturb" feature during study time.

Trello

Plan your study sessions with specific goals for each one.

Forest

Keep your phone away from your study space to avoid temptation.

Todoist

Make a to-do list every morning on your phone to keep track of tasks.

WaterMinder

Stay hydrated by setting reminders to drink water every 30 minutes.

Headspace

Practice deep breathing exercises before starting your study session.

Focus@Will

Start studying with the most difficult subject to get it out of the way.

Google Calendar

Use your phone’s calendar to plan long-term study goals.

Grayscale Mode (iOS)

Minimize distractions by keeping your phone screen dark or in grayscale mode.

Calm

Visualize the success of your studies, imagining how good you will feel after completing a task.

Spotify

Create a playlist of calming or instrumental music to listen to while studying.

Google Keep

Write down your goals for the day and keep track of your progress.

Fitbit

Reward yourself with a small treat after completing a study session.

YouTube

Surround yourself with positive influences and people who encourage you.

Focusmate

Break your study sessions into smaller, manageable chunks.

Simple Habit

Turn studying into a habit by doing it at the same time every day.

Evernote

Keep a notebook to jot down your thoughts or ideas during your study time.

Audible

Try listening to study podcasts or audiobooks to stay motivated.

Strava

Take breaks to do physical exercises like stretching or a quick walk.

StudyStack

Focus on mastering one concept at a time rather than multitasking.

Meditation.com

Stay positive even when things seem difficult, and remember that every effort counts.

MindMup

Use mind mapping to organize your thoughts and improve memory retention.
